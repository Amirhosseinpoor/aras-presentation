Step 3: Deep Dive into Information Retrieval IR Pyramid
Building up from classical IR to hybrid pipelines and evaluation.
Conceptual pyramid of what I studied:
Studied classical IR: TF-IDF, BM25, inverted indexes
Moved to dense retrieval: bi-encoders, ColBERT
Explored hybrid methods: SPLADE, BM25 + SPLADE + dense + rerankers
Learned IR evaluation metrics: MAP, nDCG, recall Goal: build trustworthy retrieval pipelines for LLM agents
